Announcements

On-Campus Orientation on September 10, 2022
On Campus Orientation will commence on September 10, 2022 (morning till evening). Parents and Students are scheduled to arrive on campus on Saturday, September 10, 2022 between 9:00 am and 4:00 pm. On the first day of on campus orientation you will be required to show the following documents at the main gate AND at the registration desk. These documents include conditional admissions offer, original CNIC or Passport and your Covid-19 Vaccination Certificate.After your documents are verified at the main gate ,you will be directed towards the free parking. After that parents and students will be taken to different venues as listed below:

  • Students are taken to the registration desks where they receive their smartcard and orientation welcome packet
  • Students who successfully get registered are taken for a campus tour in groups
  • While students are registering, their parents will be directed towards their respective welcome session.
